Mobile Convergence: Media in the Palm of Your Hand

by Nathan Barnes & Ty Scicluna

Fanzlive (2018)

We live in a world of information, and that very information has become more easily accessible with the advent of the Internet and the convergence of technology. With one device set to rule them all - the mobile phone.

The future of everything is mobile. Josh Steimle

While we once lived in a world of newspapers for daily news about the happenings of the world, the Internet has seen availability to information become a 24/7 possibility, and with the mobile phone being an internet enabled device we have entered into the age of world news and information in our pockets.

But what does this have to do with gaming?

The development in mobile technology has paved the way for not only news and social media, but also gaming on mobile devices and home consoles such as the Nintendo Switch that, like the mobile phone, is portable, unshackling the gamer from the desktop or couch and enabling gaming on the go at the user's discretion.

Nintendo Switch (Neon)

However, convergence of media is not a new thing. Early machines such as Nintendo's Game & Watch toys, the Nintendo Gameboy and other portable consoles, and the emergence and evolution of laptops demonstrate that advancements in mobile technology have continuously brought about greater and greater possibilities over the last thirty years. This begs the question of what is to come…
